# OSI/TCP Model

- 1 - **Physical** - Cabling
- 2 - **Data** - Switching, MAC Addresses
- 3 - **Network** - Routing, IP Addresses
- 4 - **Transport** - TCP/UDP
- 5 - **Session** - Session managment
- 6 - **Presentation** - WMV, JPEG, MOV
- 7 - **Application** - HTTP(s), SMTP
